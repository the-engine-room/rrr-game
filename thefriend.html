You work hard in school: grades and social capital are your priorities.
Your best friend wants to go to a football party, so you go with her. She wants to leave with some of the players.
You have a bad feeling, and you warn her — but she doesn’t listen.
Should you call her parents, and risk being labeled a snitch?

What do you do?

About the project

Roll Red Roll is a documentary film and live action role playing game investigating rape culture in small town America.
